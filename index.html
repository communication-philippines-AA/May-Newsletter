Father Bernard Holzer AA, New Manila

Presbyteral ordination of Bro. Joseph Panaguiton AA

Bro. Joseph Panaguiton from the St. Augustine community in Digos will be ordainedto the presbyterate through the imposition of the hands of Bishop Jose Tala-oc, Bishop of Kalibo and of Bishop Guillermo Afable, Bishop of Digos, on Monday, May 5, 2025 at 3:00pm at Divine Mercy Parish, Digos. He will celebrate a Thanksgiving Mass in his hometown in Aklan on May10 at 9am.
